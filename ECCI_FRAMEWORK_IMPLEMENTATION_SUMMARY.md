| **Content Focus** | Process-oriented | Benefit-oriented |
| **Credibility** | Academic jargon | Simple explanation + credibility markers |

---

## Key Principle Applied Throughout

**"Your audience IS professional and educated, but they're exhausted interpreters looking for solutions, not researchers reading a journal article."**

This principle guided all language changes:
- Maintain credibility through research-backed terminology
- Simplify explanations to focus on benefits
- Lead with what it does, not how it works
- Use technical terms sparingly and in context
