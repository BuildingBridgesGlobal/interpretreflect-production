## üí° Tone Guidelines

### ‚úÖ DO Use:
- Benefit-focused language
- Simple explanations + credibility markers
- Active voice ("Get insights" not "Insights are provided")
- Direct address ("your brain" not "the brain")
- Practical language ("practical strategies" not "strategic interventions")

### ‚ùå DON'T Use:
- Academic jargon without context
- Passive voice
- Overly technical terms
- Process descriptions instead of benefits
- Multiple technical terms in one sentence
